Entering

Taipei

進入台北

When I was 13, I left Taipei
我十三歲時離開了台北

a place people scramble to come,
一個人們爭先恐後的到來

to fit in,
並努力地適應

and to live out their dreams.
好實現夢想的地方。

I moved to another city. But it has only been after my studying abroad, not being able to visit Taipei by a 40-min car ride, that I started to think:
我搬去了另一座城市。然而直到我出國留學，去到一個 40 分鐘車程伸手不及的地方，我才開始去思考：

what is so unique about Taipei
是什麼，讓台北在所有人心中如此的特別

?

Assimilating

Taipei

吸收台北

Taipei locals are rich. Taipei locals are trendy fashionistas. Taipei locals are well-educated.
台北人有錢。台北人很時尚。台北人教育程度好。

Taipei is convenienet, fun, and basically
台北很便利、好玩，基本上就是

the BEST!
最棒的！

Those are some stereotypes of Taipei and the locals.
這些都是普遍對於台北，以及台北人的刻板印象。

As the time of stay gets longer, we realize that some things may just go as exactly expected, while some things don't.
一旦待久了，我們領會到，有些事情確實如預期般地呈現，而有些事情則不。

It is a journey
這是個旅程

of either ackowledging or breaking sterotypes.
一個不是承認就是打破刻板印象的旅程。

To prove it, I started to collect stories from people who are around my age, whom have come to Taipei for work and/or study.
為了佐證，我開始向與我差不多歲數的人們搜集故事，他們來台北讀書抑或是打拼的故事。

and this is what I found out: stereotypes of Taipei vary in different people's eyes.
這是我所發現的：不同的人對於台北竟有不同的「刻板」印象。

That's really self-contradicting.
有點自相矛盾。

But I what I also found out is: there is a common stereotype among all
但我也發現：有個大家都默認的刻板印象

—Taipei is where one is able to gain success, a place of
—台北是個能讓人成功的地方，在這裡

dream-
夢想

come-
將會

true.
實現。

Reinterpreting

Taipei

重釋台北

Perhaps, the biggest, one-and-only stereotype is that Taipei is an absolute place of dream-come-true
也許吧，那最大的、唯一的對於台北的刻板印象，就是台北是個美夢成真的聖地

as they first entered in their own processes of wayfinding, whilst observing, mimicking, catching up and abandoning.
刻在人們初來乍到的迷惘，人們在自我尋找過程中的觀察、模仿、競逐、和放蕩

Here, “dreams” are in fact very much overlapped
在這裡，「夢想」其實是有所重疊的

on a collective picture of what Taipei is, what a Taipei person is like.
重疊在人們的想像之上——台北是什麼樣？台北人又是什麼樣？

The diverse yet homogenized complexity of these stories showcases the dynamic between collective experiences and individual memories.
多樣卻又相似的這些故事，是個存在於共同體驗與個人記憶之間的拉扯。

Each story is of one unique person, but comes together as one.
每個故事，都是屬於獨一無二的那個人的。然而總著看，竟也合成了一個敘事。

Entering, assimilating, and then reinterpreting, I discovered that the experience of accommodating to a new city is conceptually aligned with the technical and poetic process of erasure poetry
進入、吸收、重釋。我發現這個適應一個新城市的過程，不論在技巧與概念上，都與刪除式詩作同軌並行

—a text being encountered, read, and eventually “erased” in forming new interpretations of an existing narrative.
—與一段文字相遇，閱讀它，然後進行「刪除」的動作，創作出一個舊有敘事的全新詮釋。

Taking the form of erasure poetry, I created Taipei Dreams,
我用刪除式詩作，創作了台北夢。

as both a social practice art project, and a power of a disseminating narration.
這是一個社會實踐藝術，也是一個以敘事發散、發酵的力量。

I, the participants, and readers are all forming new narratives and interpretations of Taipei.
我、提供故事的參與者、以及所有的讀眾，此時此刻都在建立關於台北的新的故事、創作新的詮釋。

Ｗe see,
我們凝視

experience,
經歷經驗

and erase.
並刪除擦拭。

By personally translating and erasing others’ stories whose original texts were in Traditional Chinese,
當我自身在翻譯，並擦拭人們用繁體中文寫下的故事時，

I have entered
我已進入了

a virtual space of the city of embedded dreams, and finding my very own, reinterpred from others’ real life endeavors.
一個鑲滿夢想的虛擬國度，並在這個用人們的人生追求建立起來的國度，尋找我自己的夢想。

This website not only provides a freely explorative narration, but a collection of city experiences, which does not have the magic to achieve one’s dreams, but
這個網誌不只提供了一個自由探索的故事空間，還是一個城市印象的集冊。就算他的確沒有讓人實現夢想的魔力，但

through interactive engagements invites people to reflect on their own encounters.
網誌上的互動性閱讀體驗，能邀請讀者進行自身經歷的反思。

Through Taipei Dreams, we could be closer to our dreams.
透過台北夢，我們又離各自的夢想，更近了一步。

你有什麼樣的期許呢？
